MisterMobile Image Cropper

A simple tool that automatically crops and enhances your product photos for Carousell.
Just put your photos in, double-click, and get perfect 1080×1080 images out.
Works on Windows and macOS.

What You Get

Windows — your folder should look like this:

📁 MisterMobileCropper/
   ├── MisterMobileCropper-v3.exe    ← The program (double-click this)
   ├── MM Watermark.png              ← Watermark image (don't delete!)
   ├── INSTRUCTIONS.html             ← This guide
   ├── input/                        ← Put your photos here
   └── output/                       ← Your cropped photos appear here

macOS — your folder should look like this:

📁 MisterMobileCropper/
   ├── MisterMobileCropper.app       ← The app (double-click this)
   ├── MM Watermark.png              ← Watermark image (don't delete!)
   ├── INSTRUCTIONS.html             ← This guide
   ├── input/                        ← Put your photos here
   └── output/                       ← Your cropped photos appear here

⚠ First time opening on Mac? Right-click the .app → click Open to bypass Gatekeeper.

Step-by-Step Guide

1 Put Your Photos In

Copy your product photos into the input folder.

  • You can put photos directly in input/, or organize them in subfolders.
  • Supported formats: JPG, JPEG, PNG, WebP, HEIC (iPhone photos)
  • You can put as many photos as you want — the tool processes them all.

Example:

input/
   ├── iPhone 15 Pro/
   │     ├── photo1.jpg
   │     ├── photo2.jpg
   │     └── photo3.heic
   └── Galaxy S24/
         ├── front.png
         └── back.png

2 Run the Program

Windows: Double-click MisterMobileCropper-v3.exe.

macOS: Double-click MisterMobileCropper.app. (First time? Right-click → Open.)

A console window will open. Just press Enter when it asks for a path — it will automatically use the input/ folder.

  Drag & drop a folder or image here, then press Enter.
  Or just press Enter to use the default ./input folder.

  Path: ← just press Enter

3 Wait for Processing

The tool will show a progress bar as it processes each image:

[#############------------] 52% (5/10)

==================================================
Processing: photo1.jpg
==================================================
  -> Loading image...
  -> Tight cropping to object...
  -> Normalizing backdrop...
  -> Enhancing image...
  -> Resizing to 1080x1080...
  -> Applying MM watermark...
  -> Saving: photo1.png
  [OK] Complete! (3.2s)

If any photo fails, it won't stop — it skips the problem photo and continues with the rest. At the end you'll see a summary:

  [+] Processed: 9
  [-] Failed:    1

4 Get Your Results

Open the output folder. Your cropped photos are there, ready to upload to Carousell.

  • All photos are saved as PNG files at 1080×1080 pixels.
  • Subfolder structure is preserved (e.g., output/iPhone 15 Pro/photo1.png).
  • The MisterMobile watermark is automatically added.

5 Upload to Carousell

Select all photos from output/ and upload them directly to your Carousell listing. They're already the perfect size and format.

Alternative: Drag & Drop a Folder

Instead of using the default input/ folder, you can drag any folder directly onto the console window:

  1. Double-click the .exe (Windows) or .app (macOS)
  2. When it asks for a path, drag a folder from File Explorer / Finder into the console window
  3. Press Enter

This is handy if your photos are on a USB drive or somewhere else.

Troubleshooting

"No images found"
→ Make sure your photos are inside the input/ folder (not in a deeper nested folder you didn't expect).
Photos look the same / not cropped
→ The tool needs a product on a background to detect. Plain flat-lay photos with no distinct object may not crop well.
Watermark is missing
→ Check that MM Watermark.png is in the same folder as the .exe / .app.
The program closes immediately (Windows)
→ Try running from Command Prompt instead: open cmd, navigate to the folder, and type MisterMobileCropper-v3.exe. This way you can see any error messages.
"App is damaged" or won't open (macOS)
→ Right-click the .app → click Open. macOS blocks unsigned apps by default — this bypasses Gatekeeper for this app only.
"Path not found" error
→ Make sure the path you entered/dragged exists and doesn't have special characters.

What the Tool Does to Your Photos

  1. Detects your product using AI (removes background clutter from crop)
  2. Crops tightly around the product with a small padding
  3. Brightens the background to a clean, consistent white
  4. Enhances contrast and sharpness slightly
  5. Resizes to a perfect 1080×1080 square
  6. Adds the MisterMobile watermark
  7. Saves as a high-quality PNG
